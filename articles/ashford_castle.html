Ashford Castle

County Mayo, Ireland, Ireland

1228 (expanded 1800s)

Overview

Dating from 1228, Ashford Castle evolved from a Norman stone fortress into one of Ireland's most luxurious castle hotels. Set on the shores of Lough Corrib, this 800-year-old castle combines medieval heritage with modern luxury hospitality.
