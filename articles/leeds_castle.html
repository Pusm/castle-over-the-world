Leeds Castle

Kent, England, England

1119 onwards

Overview

Known as the 'loveliest castle in the world,' Leeds Castle sits on two islands in a lake formed by the River Len. Once home to six medieval queens, this romantic castle combines Norman origins with later comfortable residential additions and stunning gardens.
