Overview

Country: Egypt

Location: Alexandria, Egypt

Architectural Style: Mamluk military architecture

Year Built: 1477-1479

Built by Mamluk Sultan Qaitbay on the exact site of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria (one of the Seven Wonders), this fortress guarded Alexandria's harbor for centuries. The citadel incorporates stones from the fallen lighthouse into its construction.

Key Features

  • Built on site of ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria
  • Incorporates stones from the fallen lighthouse
  • Mamluk military architecture
  • Guards Alexandria's harbor
  • Built by Sultan Qaitbay
  • Museum of naval history
