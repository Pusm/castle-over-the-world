Citadel of Qaitbay

Alexandria, Egypt, Egypt

1477-1479

Overview

Built by Mamluk Sultan Qaitbay on the exact site of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria (one of the Seven Wonders), this fortress guarded Alexandria's harbor for centuries. The citadel incorporates stones from the fallen lighthouse into its construction.
