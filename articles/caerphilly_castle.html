Caerphilly Castle

Caerphilly, South Wales, Wales

1268-1271

Overview

Sprawling over 30 acres, Caerphilly Castle is the largest castle in Wales and the second-largest in Britain. This masterpiece of medieval military engineering features an elaborate system of moats and fortifications, with its leaning tower that out-leans Pisa's famous tower.
