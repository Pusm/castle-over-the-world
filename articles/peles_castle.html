Peleș Castle

Sinaia, Prahova County, Romania

1873-1914

Overview

Built as a summer retreat for King Carol I of Romania, Peleș Castle is considered one of Europe's most beautiful castles. This architectural masterpiece in the Carpathian Mountains combines Neo-Renaissance style with Art Nouveau elements and features the first European castle with central heating and electricity.
