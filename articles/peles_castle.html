Peleș Castle

← Back to Castle Index

Overview

Country: Romania

Location: Sinaia, Prahova County

Architectural Style: Neo-Renaissance with Art Nouveau elements

Year Built: 1873-1914

Built as a summer retreat for King Carol I of Romania, Peleș Castle is considered one of Europe's most beautiful castles. This architectural masterpiece in the Carpathian Mountains combines Neo-Renaissance style with Art Nouveau elements and features the first European castle with central heating and electricity.

Key Features

  • Summer retreat of King Carol I
  • One of Europe's most beautiful castles
  • Carpathian Mountains setting
  • First castle with central heating
  • First castle with electricity
  • Neo-Renaissance and Art Nouveau blend
