Overview

Country: Germany

Location: Rhineland-Palatinate

Architectural Style: Medieval hill castle

Year Built: 1101-1200

One of the few castles in the Rhineland that has never been destroyed, Burg Eltz has been owned by the same family for over 850 years. Nestled in a valley and surrounded by forest, this perfectly preserved medieval castle represents authentic German castle architecture spanning 500 years of development.
