Overview

Country: Morocco

Location: Ouarzazate Province, Morocco

Architectural Style: Traditional Moroccan earthen architecture

Year Built: 1001-1100

This fortified village (ksar) along the former caravan route between the Sahara and Marrakech represents traditional pre-Saharan habitat. Built with mud brick, clay, and straw, it's one of Morocco's most photographed locations and a popular filming location.

Key Features

  • Traditional pre-Saharan architecture
  • Built with mud brick, clay, and straw
  • Former caravan route fortress
  • Popular filming location
  • Traditional Berber architecture
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1987
