Ksar of Aït Benhaddou

Ouarzazate Province, Morocco, Morocco

11th century onwards

Overview

This fortified village (ksar) along the former caravan route between the Sahara and Marrakech represents traditional pre-Saharan habitat. Built with mud brick, clay, and straw, it's one of Morocco's most photographed locations and a popular filming location.
