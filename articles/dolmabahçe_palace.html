Dolmabahçe Palace

Istanbul, Turkey, Turkey

1843-1856

Overview

Built to rival European palaces, Dolmabahçe served as the main administrative center of the Ottoman Empire in its final century. This opulent palace along the Bosphorus represents the Westernization of Ottoman architecture and the empire's attempt to modernize while maintaining imperial grandeur.
