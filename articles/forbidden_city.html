Forbidden City

Beijing, China, China

1406-1420 (Ming Dynasty)

Overview

The imperial palace complex that served as home to 24 Ming and Qing dynasty emperors for over 500 years. Covering 720,000 square meters with 980 buildings, it's the world's most-visited palace and represents the pinnacle of traditional Chinese palatial architecture.
