Hohensalzburg Fortress

Salzburg, Austria, Austria

1077 onwards

Overview

One of the largest medieval castles in Europe, Hohensalzburg Fortress dominates Salzburg's skyline and has never been successfully besieged. Built by the Prince-Archbishops of Salzburg, this fortress represents over 900 years of continuous architectural development and strategic importance.
