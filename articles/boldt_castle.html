Boldt Castle

Heart Island, New York, United States

1900-1904 (unfinished)

Overview

Hotel magnate George C. Boldt began building this 120-room castle as a summer home for his beloved wife Louise. When she died before completion, construction halted for 70 years until restoration began, creating one of the most romantic castle stories in America.
