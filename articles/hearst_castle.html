Hearst Castle

San Simeon, California, United States

1919-1947

Overview

Built by newspaper tycoon William Randolph Hearst and architect Julia Morgan, this extravagant estate nicknamed 'La Cuesta Encantada' (The Enchanted Hill) features 165 rooms, world-class art collections, and 127 acres of terraced gardens overlooking the Pacific Coast.
