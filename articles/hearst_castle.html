Overview

Country: United States

Location: San Simeon, California

Architectural Style: Mediterranean Revival

Year Built: 1919-1947

Built by newspaper tycoon William Randolph Hearst and architect Julia Morgan, this extravagant estate nicknamed 'La Cuesta Encantada' (The Enchanted Hill) features 165 rooms, world-class art collections, and 127 acres of terraced gardens overlooking the Pacific Coast.

Key Features

  • 165 rooms in four buildings
  • 127 acres of terraced gardens
  • World-class art collections
  • Designed by Julia Morgan
  • Neptune Pool and Roman Pool
  • California Historical Landmark
