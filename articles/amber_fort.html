Amber Fort

Jaipur, Rajasthan, India

1592 onwards

Overview

Perched on a hilltop overlooking Maota Lake, Amber Fort is a magnificent example of Rajput architecture blended with Mughal influences. Known for its artistic Hindu-style elements and stunning mirror work in the Sheesh Mahal, it represents the golden age of Rajasthani palace architecture.
