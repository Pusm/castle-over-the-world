Overview

Country: Syria

Location: Homs Governorate, Syria

Architectural Style: Crusader castle architecture

Year Built: 1142-1271

One of the most important preserved medieval castles in the world, Krak des Chevaliers was built by the Knights Hospitaller during the Crusades. This massive fortress represents the pinnacle of medieval military architecture and engineering in the Middle East.

Key Features

  • Most important preserved medieval castle worldwide
  • Built by Knights Hospitaller
  • Crusader military architecture masterpiece
  • Massive defensive walls and towers
  • Strategic location controlling trade routes
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site since 2006
