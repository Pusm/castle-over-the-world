Bannerman Castle

Pollepel Island, Hudson River, New York, United States

1901-1918

Overview

Built by arms dealer Francis Bannerman VI as an ammunition storage facility and summer residence, this Scottish-style castle with turrets, towers, and a moat was largely destroyed by fire but remains a haunting ruin accessible only by boat tours through the Hudson River.
