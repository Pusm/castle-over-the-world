Overview

Country: Romania

Location: Hunedoara, Hunedoara County

Architectural Style: Gothic-Renaissance

Year Built: 14th-15th centuries

Built by John Hunyadi, regent of Hungary and father of Matthias Corvinus, this fairy-tale Gothic castle is one of the largest in Europe. With its impressive towers, drawbridge, and Gothic architecture, Hunyadi Castle represents the pinnacle of medieval Transylvanian fortification design.

Key Features
