Gillette Castle

East Haddam, Connecticut, United States

1914-1919

Overview

This zany, medieval-inspired fortress was dreamed up by William Gillette, famous playwright and Sherlock Holmes actor. Built as his retirement home, the castle features 47 unique doors, secret passages, hidden rooms, and thousands of hand-crafted details that reflect Gillette's theatrical personality.
