Karlštejn Castle

Overview

Country: Czech Republic

Location: Karlštejn, Central Bohemian Region

Architectural Style: Gothic

Year Built: 1348-1365

Founded by Charles IV, Holy Roman Emperor and King of Bohemia, Karlštejn was built to safeguard the imperial regalia and holy relics. This Gothic castle represents the 'castliest castle of them all' with its fairy-tale appearance and perfectly preserved medieval interiors including the stunning Chapel of the Holy Cross.

Key Features

  • Built for Charles IV, Holy Roman Emperor
  • Treasury for imperial regalia and holy relics
  • Chapel of the Holy Cross with precious stones
  • Gothic architecture masterpiece
  • Fairy-tale castle appearance
  • Crown Jewels replica display
