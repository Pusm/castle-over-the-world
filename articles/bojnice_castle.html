Bojnice Castle

← Back to Castle Index

Overview

Country: Slovakia

Location: Bojnice, Trenčín Region

Architectural Style: Romantic castle (reconstruction)

Year Built: 1100-1200

Slovakia's most visited castle and often called the most beautiful castle in Slovakia, Bojnice was romantically rebuilt by Count János Ferenc Pálffy to resemble the châteaux of the Loire Valley. This fairy-tale castle now serves as a museum and popular filming location.

Key Features

  • Most visited castle in Slovakia
  • Romantic reconstruction inspired by Loire Valley
  • Built by Count János Ferenc Pálffy
  • Fairy-tale appearance
  • Popular filming location
  • Museum and cultural events venue
