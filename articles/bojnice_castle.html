Bojnice Castle

Bojnice, Trenčín Region, Slovakia

12th century (rebuilt 1889-1910)

Overview

Slovakia's most visited castle and often called the most beautiful castle in Slovakia, Bojnice was romantically rebuilt by Count János Ferenc Pálffy to resemble the châteaux of the Loire Valley. This fairy-tale castle now serves as a museum and popular filming location.
