Overview

Country: Japan

Location: Matsumoto, Nagano Prefecture

Architectural Style: Japanese hirajiro (flatland castle)

Year Built: 1592-1614

Known as 'Crow Castle' due to its striking black exterior, Matsumoto is one of Japan's most complete original castles. This National Treasure showcases sophisticated Japanese castle design with its distinctive black and white facade and complex defensive layout.

Key Features

  • Known as 'Crow Castle'
  • Striking black exterior
  • One of Japan's 12 original castles
  • National Treasure of Japan
  • Complex six-story keep
  • Moated flatland design
