Matsumoto Castle

Matsumoto, Nagano Prefecture, Japan

1592-1614

Overview

Known as 'Crow Castle' due to its striking black exterior, Matsumoto is one of Japan's most complete original castles. This National Treasure showcases sophisticated Japanese castle design with its distinctive black and white facade and complex defensive layout.

Basic Information

Architectural Style:
Japanese hirajiro (flatland castle)
