Spiš Castle

Spišské Podhradie, Slovakia, Slovakia

12th century onwards

Overview

One of the largest castle complexes in the world, Spiš Castle spreads over 4 hectares atop a travertine hill. This UNESCO World Heritage site represents 800 years of architectural development and served as an important center of power in medieval Hungary.
