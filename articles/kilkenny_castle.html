Kilkenny Castle

Kilkenny, Ireland, Ireland

1195 onwards

Overview

Built by the Norman-Welsh nobleman William Marshal, Kilkenny Castle dominated the crossing of the River Nore for over 800 years. The seat of the powerful Butler family, this castle represents the Norman conquest of Ireland and subsequent Irish aristocratic life.
