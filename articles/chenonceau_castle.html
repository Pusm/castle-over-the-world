Château de Chenonceau

Chenonceaux, Indre-et-Loire, Loire Valley, France

1514-1522

Overview

Known as the 'Ladies' Castle' due to the influential women who shaped its history, Chenonceau spans the River Cher with its distinctive arched bridge gallery. This elegant château represents the height of French Renaissance architecture and is the most visited château in France after Versailles.
