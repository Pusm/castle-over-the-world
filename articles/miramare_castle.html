Overview

Country: Italy

Location: Trieste, Friuli-Venezia Giulia

Architectural Style: Eclectic 19th-century romantic

Year Built: 1856-1860

Built by Archduke Ferdinand Maximilian of Austria on a rocky promontory overlooking the Adriatic Sea, Miramare Castle combines romantic castle architecture with stunning coastal setting. The tragic story of Maximilian's execution in Mexico adds poignancy to this architectural gem.
