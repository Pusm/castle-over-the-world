Overview

Country: England

Location: Dorset, England

Architectural Style: Norman medieval ruins

Year Built: 1086

One of Britain's most iconic ruined castles, Corfe Castle stands dramatically on a natural hill controlling the gap in the Purbeck Hills. Slighted during the English Civil War, its romantic ruins have inspired artists and writers for centuries, representing the archetypal English castle ruin.
