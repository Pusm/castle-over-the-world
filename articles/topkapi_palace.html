Topkapi Palace

Istanbul, Turkey, Turkey

1459-1478

Overview

The primary residence of the Ottoman sultans for nearly 400 years, Topkapi Palace overlooks the Bosphorus and Golden Horn. This vast complex of courtyards, pavilions, and gardens represents the pinnacle of Ottoman palace architecture and housed the heart of the Ottoman Empire.
