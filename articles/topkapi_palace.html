Overview

Country: Turkey

Location: Istanbul, Turkey

Architectural Style: Ottoman palace architecture

Year Built: 1459-1478

The primary residence of the Ottoman sultans for nearly 400 years, Topkapi Palace overlooks the Bosphorus and Golden Horn. This vast complex of courtyards, pavilions, and gardens represents the pinnacle of Ottoman palace architecture and housed the heart of the Ottoman Empire.

Key Features

  • Primary residence of Ottoman sultans
  • Overlooks Bosphorus and Golden Horn
  • 400 years of Ottoman imperial history
  • Multiple courtyards and pavilions
  • Imperial Treasury and collections
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
