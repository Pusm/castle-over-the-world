Belém Tower

Lisbon, Portugal, Portugal

1514-1520

Overview

Built to defend Lisbon's harbor and commemorate Vasco da Gama's expedition, this elegant tower represents the Manueline style at its finest. The tower served as both fortress and ceremonial gateway to Lisbon during Portugal's Age of Discovery.
