Château de Chantilly

Chantilly, Oise, Hauts-de-France, France

14th century (rebuilt 1870s-1880s)

Overview

Home to the Duke of Aumale and containing the second-largest collection of antique paintings in France after the Louvre, Chantilly combines Renaissance architecture with 19th-century reconstruction. The château is famous for its magnificent stables and art collections.
