Overview

Country: Slovenia

Location: Postojna, Inner Carniola

Architectural Style: Renaissance castle built into cliff face

Year Built: 1500-1600

Built dramatically into the mouth of a cave within a 123-meter-high cliff, Predjama Castle is listed in the Guinness Book of Records as the largest cave castle in the world. This architectural marvel seamlessly integrates human construction with natural rock formations.

Key Features

  • Largest cave castle in the world
  • Built into 123-meter cliff face
  • Integrates with natural cave system
  • Renaissance reconstruction
  • Guinness World Record holder
  • Secret tunnel to cave system
