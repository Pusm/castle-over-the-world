Overview

Country: Romania

Location: Bran, Brasov County

Architectural Style: Gothic Revival

Year Built: 1377-1388

Often referred to as 'Dracula's Castle', this medieval fortress sits dramatically on a rocky outcrop. While Bram Stoker likely never visited, the castle's Gothic appearance made it the perfect inspiration for Count Dracula's Transylvanian home.
