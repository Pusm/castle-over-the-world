Red Fort (Lal Qila)

Delhi, India, India

1639-1648

Overview

Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as the fortified palace of Shahjahanabad, the Red Fort served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years. Its red sandstone walls and sophisticated Mughal architecture represent the zenith of Indo-Islamic architectural achievement.
