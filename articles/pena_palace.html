Pena Palace

Sintra, Portugal, Portugal

1842-1854

Overview

One of Europe's most fantastical palaces, Pena Palace crowns the hills of Sintra with its colorful romantic architecture blending Gothic, Renaissance, Moorish, and Manueline styles. Built for King Ferdinand II, it represents the Portuguese romantic movement and showcases architectural eclecticism.
