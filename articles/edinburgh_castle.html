Edinburgh Castle

Edinburgh, Scotland, Scotland

Overview

Perched dramatically on Castle Rock, Edinburgh Castle has dominated Scotland's capital for nearly two millennia. This ancient fortress guards the Scottish Crown Jewels and embodies the turbulent history of Scotland, serving as both royal residence and military stronghold throughout the centuries.

Castle Information

Architectural Style:
Medieval Scottish fortress (multiple periods)
Year Built:
12th century (on 2nd century foundations)
Location:
Edinburgh, Scotland, Scotland

Key Features

  • Honours of Scotland (oldest crown jewels)
  • Castle Rock volcanic foundation
  • Great Hall with impressive timber roof
  • St Margaret's Chapel (oldest building)
  • One O'Clock Gun daily ceremony
  • Spectacular views over Edinburgh
